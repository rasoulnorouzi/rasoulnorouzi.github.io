Avoid using em dashes (---) and "AI sign" phrasing in any writing.

**Why:** User preference for cleaner, more natural writing style.
**How to apply:** When writing or editing any content (project descriptions, blog posts, README, etc.), use regular dashes or commas instead of em dashes. Avoid phrases like "AI-powered" or similar "AI sign" language.
