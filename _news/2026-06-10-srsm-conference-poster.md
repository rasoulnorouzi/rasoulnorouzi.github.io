Presenting a poster at the <a href="https://www.srsm.org/"><strong>SRSM Annual Meeting 2026</strong></a> in Chania, Crete, Greece (June 10–12). Poster title: <em>From Causal Extraction to Concept Harmonization: A Computational Pipeline for Automated Qualitative Research Synthesis</em>. The work presents a BERT-based multitask model for parsing causal relations from social science publications (matching human inter-rater reliability, Krippendorff's alpha = 0.80) and a multidimensional benchmark for concept harmonization methods. Together, these studies move toward a full pipeline for automated research synthesis.
